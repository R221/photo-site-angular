    Frequently Asked Questions


    What kinds of events do you shoot?
    Weddings, engagements, baby showers, graduation photos, work/holiday parties, hotdog eating contests. You know, the ususal.

    How would you describe your style?
    I love capturing candid moments. Frame and colorwise, it really depends on the subject and location but I tend to gravitate toward a more cinematic feel. I also love applying a retro look when appropriate. So a fuller color. I also do B&W and film variants.

    Are you the photographer for me?
    Yes, most likely. If you want pictures, then definitely. If you want video, then probably not.

    Who is your typical customer?
    My core customers are families, individuals, and event planners that are hosting parties, large or small, and celebrations. I especially love shooting engagements and baby showers/gender reveals because there's generally a certain element of surprise that makes for wonderful facial expressions. And most people these days do these events at nice locations, so it makes the job that much easier.
